How to Play Friday Night Funkin

Playing Friday Night Funkin is simple but mastering it takes practice. Use the arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) to match the rhythm and hit the notes as they reach the judgment line. Watch Boyfriend's side of the screen and press the corresponding arrow key when the arrows align with the targets at the top.

Your performance is measured by your accuracy and timing. Miss too many notes and you'll lose the battle! Each song has its own unique pattern and difficulty level, starting easy and gradually becoming more challenging as you progress through the weeks. Perfect your timing, learn the patterns, and show everyone that you've got the skills to win Girlfriend's heart!
